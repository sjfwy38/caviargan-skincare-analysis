## Why These Three Ingredients Together?

This is the part most reviews skip over, and it's actually worth understanding.

### Argan Oil

Pure Moroccan argan oil is packed with Vitamin E, essential fatty acids, and antioxidants. It hydrates deeply without feeling greasy, and its antioxidant profile helps defend against environmental damage — the UV exposure that's responsible for most visible aging. On its own, argan oil is a solid moisturizer. But it doesn't resurface skin or boost collagen.

### Caviar Extract

This is where it gets interesting. Caviar extract — refined from fish roe — is loaded with omega fatty acids, proteins, and amino acids. In skincare, it's used to plump skin by increasing hydration at a deeper level, smooth texture, and support skin's natural renewal. Some studies suggest it also has mild antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties.

### Retinyl Palmitate

Retinol is the most studied anti-aging ingredient that doesn't require a prescription. It accelerates cell turnover, boosts collagen production, and reduces the appearance of fine lines over time. Retinyl palmitate is the ester form — slower to convert, but significantly gentler. For mature skin that can react badly to straight retinol, that's a meaningful distinction.

The synergy argument: argan oil locks in moisture and protects the skin barrier, caviar extract plumps and replenishes, retinyl palmitate resurfaces and rebuilds. Each ingredient addresses a different mechanism of aging. That's why combining them makes more sense than using any one alone.

CaviArgan's formula also includes Matrixyl Synthe'6 (a collagen-boosting peptide), hyaluronic acid, Vitamin C, Vitamin E, and green tea extract — rounding out what is, for a single cream, a genuinely dense ingredient list.
